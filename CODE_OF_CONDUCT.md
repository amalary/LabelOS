# Code of Conduct

Label OS should be a professional, respectful, and collaborative project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while staying accountable for impact.
- Keep feedback specific, actionable, and focused on the work.
- Respect privacy, confidentiality, and legal obligations around music industry data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks.
- Sharing private information without permission.
- Introducing secrets, production data, or confidential material into the repository.
- Disruptive conduct that prevents productive collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content, close issues or pull requests, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code.
